About

I'm a second year PhD student in the Visual Geometry Group at Oxford University, co-supervised by Prof. Andrew Zisserman and Prof. Andrea Vedaldi. My research mainly focuses on image recognition and on open-set recognition (teaching deep networks to recognises images which are semantically different to those they have seen during training). My research is funded by the Facebook AI Research Scholarship.

I previously studied Engineering Science, also at Oxford, graduating in 2018. In between graduating and beginning my PhD, I was fortunate to work at CuriousAI in Helsinki and Founders Factory in London. I have since continued to work with start-ups when time allows.
